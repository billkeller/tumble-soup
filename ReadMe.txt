/*--------------------------------------------------
To install:
---------------------------------------------------*/
Click on 'Customize' in the top right corner of your Tumblr blog

Click on the 'Edit HTML' button in the Custom theme area

Copy and past the contents of theme.html into the editor

Click on 'Update preview', then 'Appearance', then 'Save'

View your Tumblr blog
